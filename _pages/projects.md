## Visual Inertial Teach and Repeat
**Edge AI, Computer Vision** | April 2026

Built a lightweight vision-based teach-and-repeat navigation system on Jetson Nano, integrating monocular perception, visual localization, and Ackermann Pure-Pursuit control for autonomous route following.

- Designed an ORB + Lucas–Kanade tracking module for visual relocalization and heading correction.
- Investigated trade-offs between feature-based and appearance-based navigation under low-texture and dynamic-scene conditions.
- Analyzed drift, lateral error, route completion robustness, and resource utilization on embedded hardware.
